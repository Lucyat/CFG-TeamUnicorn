Canada

Canada is country in North America, with ten provinces and three territories extend from the Atlantic Ocean to the Pacific Ocean and northward into the Artic Ocean. Canada covers 9.98 million square kilometres, which makes it the worlds second-largest country by total area. I have family who have migrated to Canada.

Canada has so much to see, so many National Parks and Reservations and the Government are planning on openning new Parks. There is a lot of snow and some provinces like Alberta which is near the mountains can reach temperatures of -22. In rural provinces it is not strange to see Bear and Cyote warnings especially before Bears are due to go into hibernation.
Canada has so many delicious food to taste. The Canadians are well known for their Maple Syrup and they apparently have the best bacon in the world! Canada has a huge amount of food to taste with each province having their own signature dishes.
Alberta is located in Western Canada, in the province of Alberta. Surrounded by loads of rocky mountains and vast prairies. Lots of natural wildlife such as grizzly bear and the wolf.
